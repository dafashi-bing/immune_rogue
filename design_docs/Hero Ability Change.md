# Hero Ability Change  
Version: 1.0  
Date: 2025-11-01  
Author: [TO-DO]

---

## 1. Circle – “The Wanderer” → Macrophage  
• Base Ability: Engulf Surge  
  – Lunge forward up to 200 units, dealing 40 damage to each enemy passed through and briefly staggering them. (6 s cooldown)  
• Ultimate: Engulf Rampage  
  – For 3 s, dash continuously, bouncing off battlefield edges. Any enemy you collide with is instantly devoured (instant kill).  

## 2. Triangle – “The Precision Strike” → Plasma Cell  
• Base Ability: Antibody Lance  
  – Fire one high-velocity, piercing antibody projectile that travels the entire screen and deals 999 damage on hit. (5 s cooldown)  
• Ultimate: Immunoglobulin Barrage  
  – Over 3 s, unleash a 360° rapid-fire spray of 20 antibodies (≈6 per second). Each deals 60 damage and applies a 30% slow for 2 s. Movement speed is reduced by 50% while channeling.  

## 3. Square – “The Commander” → Helper T-cell  
• Base Ability: Cytokine Burst  
  – Emit a pulse in a 150-unit radius, dealing 25 damage and stunning enemies for 1.5 s. (7 s cooldown)  
• Ultimate: Cytokine Cataclysm  
  – Instantly blanket the entire battlefield in a raging cytokine storm for 3 s. Every 0.5 s, all enemies take 50 damage and are stunned for 1 s. At the end of the storm, gain a 5 s buff: +30% damage reduction.  

## 4. Pentagon – “The Explosive Artist” → Neutrophil  
• Base Ability: NET Flare  
  – Release a radial burst of five NET projectiles, each dealing 30 damage and slowing hit enemies by 30% for 2 s. (8 s cooldown)  
• Ultimate: NET Tsunami  
  – For 4 s, create a 300-unit swirling NET zone that deals 20 damage/sec and stacks slows (up to 40%). Enemies entering the zone trigger explosive NET blasts for 100 damage each.  

## 5. Hexagon – “The Tactical Genius” → Dendritic Cell  
• Base Ability: Antigen Web  
  – Spawn a static 200-unit zone that deals 10 damage/sec and slows enemies by 30% for 4 s. (9 s cooldown)  
• Ultimate: Antigen Storm  
  – Over 3 s, call down 6 radial “dendrite strikes.” Each strike spawns a mini-web (100-unit radius) that deals 40 damage and roots enemies for 1 s. The combined zone lingers for 2 s afterward.  

## 6. Heptagon – “The Resonant Controller” → Cytotoxic T-cell  
• Base Ability: Perforin Punch  
  – Send out a ring blast at 100-unit range, dealing 25 damage and knocking enemies back 30 units. (10 s cooldown)  
• Ultimate: Apoptotic Exterminatus  
  – Unleash an ever-expanding perforin ring with unlimited range. Any enemy it touches is forced outward toward the map border; upon hitting the border, they are instantly eliminated.
